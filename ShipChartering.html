Ship Chartering

Rassoula Shipping has separate Broking Desk, which acts as an intermediary between the cargo provider (i.e. charterer) and the shipping company (i.e. ship owner). With good understanding of market, our brokers are able to guide accurately on prevailing freight rates which helps traders to make sales contract. Starting with negotiation and fixing, our job includes preparing recap, charter party and laytime calculations, following final payments to close the file. These are the vital parts of our service. Our broking division is working very closely with extensive number of ship owners and charterers. Please email your requirements at: chartering@rassoula.com

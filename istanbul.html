Ship Agency Istanbul


We offer a full range of agency services at all major Turkish Ports, Turkish Straits as well as during dry-docking and repairs at Shipyards in Tuzla and Yalova regions. We work closely with harbor masters, local authorities and service providers

To achieve prompt attention to vessels with minimum delays and cost to the Owner. We ensure that your interests are well taken care of to avoid any conflict of interest that may arise with many stakeholders involved in various operations.

FULL PORT AGENCY

Logistics Services

Dry-docking Agency
